---
title: Prompting Principles
tags: [ai, prompting]
created: 2026-06-12
---

Distilled from building things with LLMs.

1. **Show, don't describe.** One good example beats three paragraphs of rules.
2. **Constrain the output.** Schemas, formats, length limits.
3. **Give it an out.** "Say 'unknown' if unsure" kills most hallucinations.
4. **Iterate on failures.** Save bad outputs, turn them into test cases.

These compound when building [[Agents and Tool Use]].
